WHAT WE DO :

TGCMI RESEARCH:

We conduct research on the known and well-documented causes of women’s under-development. We also document and disseminate BEST PRACTICES from Nigeria, Africa and across the globe of strategies that have worked and moved women forward. We deplore, document and disseminate TRIED & TESTED approaches, programs, outcomes and lessons learned about women’s development from around the world and how these might be adapted to Nigeria’s situation.

TGCMI PUBLICATIONS:

We publish books, magazines and other media for and by women and girls as platforms and to give a voice to girls and women to ventilate their issues. These include a MENTORS’ MANUAL, GIRLS’ ZONE, GIRLS’ OWN and others.

TGCMI RESOURCE CENTRES:

This very vital resource for the millions of girls who have little or no access to life-skill and life-saving information and starts with the establishment of MINI LIBRARIES in schools, churches, Mosques and neighborhoods. It then builds on to include computers and the Internet for research and networking by the girls themselves.

GIRL MENTORS’ CLUBS:

This is our flagship project that brings together carefully selected, committed and trained mentors with girls and young women in schools, churches, Mosques and youth organizations for the purpose of grooming girls to become confident, strong, smart and successful, achieve their potentials in life and deploy these to family, society, organizations, and the nations. In addition we are linked to a network and maintain a roster of cosultants and resource persons whom we draw on according to need.

PUBLIC ENLIGHTENMENT AND CIVIC EDUCATION

We engage in face-to-face community action and social mobilization activities, public enlightenment, civic education, awareness creation and sensitization for In-school and Out-of-School youth using various communication channels including songs, drama, community dialogue, town hall meetings, debates, to increase awareness and knowledge of health and social issues.

PRODUCTION OF AUDIO-VISUAL MATERIALS

In realization of the need for improved relationship between men and women, we produce informational and educational online and offline audio-visual materials for boys and girls, and men and women, including short audio and video clips, musicals, Drama series, Radio and TV Talk shows and other materials as part of efforts to bridge the gap in understanding between men and women, and to increase learning for young people on entrepreneurship, vocational, life and social skills.

GIRLS’ SUMMITS

We conduct Girls’ Summits, Dialogues, Seminars, etc, for the purpose of training Mentors, Big Sisters Aunties and linking them with needy girls.

MONITORING AND EVALUATION

We conduct internal monitoring and evaluation, as well as benefit from external peer reviews of our projects.

NETWORKING AND COLLABORATION

We network and collaborate with governments, NGOs, schools, churches, Mosques, Youth Organizations, Research Firms, Consultants in our quest to improve the life goals of girls/women, and in deploying women’s contributions to social & national development.
