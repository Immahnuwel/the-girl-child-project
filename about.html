WHO WE ARE:

THE GIRL CHILD MENTORING INITIATIVE, TCGMI

is a cutting edge, dynamic Civil Society Organization, set up to re-position the issues surrounding Optimum Human Development from the postion of building the talents, skills and capacities of half of the Human Race, i.e. the female of the spieces, for national and global development.

We are a network of credible, concerned, and committed

individual men and women from every strata of society bound together by the dogged determination to re-examine, research, and bring fresh understanding to the issues of Nigeria and Africa’s under-development from the perspective of its hitherto un-tapped, un-exploited resources of half of its population, the women.

We believe that no society can accelerrate its development if

it misunderstands, ignores and fails to deploy the contributions of 50% of its population, and half of its available human resources. We continue to waste and under-utilize the gifts, talents, resources and capacities of our girls, and women at our own peril and might actually be in danger of retrogression, stagnation and failure to catch up with the mass of humanity in this post-jet age of the 21st century and into the future. TGCMI is determined to find our place in the bright future that awaits Nigeria as a projected member of the 20 most developed economies in the world by maximally training, deploying and harnessing the capacities of girls and women. We accomplish this through RESEARCH, ADVOCACY, MOBILIZING, TRAINING, MENTORING AND DEPLOYING THE BEST CAPACITIES of the next generation of Nigerian women and girls.

  • OUR VISION:
  • OUR MISSION:
  • OUR INSPIRATION:

A Nigeria of the future in which generations of strong, smart and successful girls and women have been mentored and empowered and do realize the full expression of their God-given and highest potentials in life.
A country in which 50% of our population can freely deploy and contribute their best capacities to solve national challenges and thereby accelerate the development of the huge material and human resources of our country and continent.
A country in which women feel safe and secure, are appreciated, their human rights are respected, and in which they can freely express initiative, self-reliance, courage, and engage in productive social & financial enterprise.

To provide girls and women with materials, training, books and other resources in order to discover their gifts and talents and determine their life goals. To mentor girls and women through friendship, encouragement, counseling and support directly, through linkages with Big Sisters & Aunties and through outreaches in schools, churches, Mosques, and Youth Organizations.

We are inspired by the fact that over time women have demonstrated their capacity to assume effective leadership of countries, multi-national organizations, in science, technology and in every imaginable area of human endeavour. We are inspired by womens’ ability to step over obstacles and challenges of disempowering traditions, customs, religious, lack of education, poor access to opportunities to reach their goals. We are inspired by the mounting evidence of women’s achievements and contributions to social and national development even in Africa, especially in Nigeria. .
